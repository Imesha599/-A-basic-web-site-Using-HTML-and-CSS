SWIMMING POOL

Glory Hotel’s swimming pool commands spectacular views of the Indian Ocean and the busy streets of the Matara Road. Located on the 1st floor of the Hotel, the pool side presents a tranquil atmosphere bathed by the rich tropical sun and the refreshing sea breeze to help you unwind in an relaxing atmosphere.Furthermore, tasteful cane furniture improves the calming atmosphere of the guest rooms with soothing lighting and a sleek interior that attracts relaxation.


With a kiddie’s pool, therapeutic massage center, gymnasium,steam room, sauna and tennis courts right adjacent, it’s the ideal venue for a relaxing day with family, . Take a splash and relax in the swimming pool overlooking the stately old parliament and the busy streets of Matara with the Ocean breeze calming your nerves reviving your spirit.
